Welcome to VTT Editor

This tool lets you edit WebVTT subtitle files in sync with a video.

Get started: Open a video file and a VTT file using the toolbar buttons, or drag and drop them onto the page. You can also click the empty side panel to open a VTT file, or the timeline bar at the bottom to open a video.

Editing: Click a cue in the list to jump to it. Edit text in place. Adjust start and end times by clicking the timecodes in the list, or drag the cue blocks on the timeline. Use the zoom controls to work precisely on long videos.

Shortcuts: Space or S play/pause. A/D or arrow keys step frame by frame. ⌘S saves.

Tip: Theme follows your system; use the ☀/🌙 button to switch. Click the "VTT Editor" title anytime to show this message again.
